Affiliate links on Hearthpick are identified by the following: product cards marked "Shop Now" link directly to retailer pages and may contain affiliate tracking codes; links within blog posts directing to external retailers may be affiliate links; we include rel="noopener sponsored" on affiliate links per Google's webmaster guidelines.

Our Editorial Independence

Affiliate relationships do not influence our editorial decisions. Specifically: we do not accept payment from retailers to feature their products; we do not guarantee positive coverage in exchange for commissions; products are selected based on quality, value, and relevance — not commission rate; we remove or update recommendations if a product's quality declines; we may feature products from retailers with whom we have no affiliate relationship.

Prices shown on Hearthpick are approximate and may change without notice. Always verify the current price on the retailer's website before purchase. We are not responsible for pricing discrepancies.

No Additional Cost to You

When you purchase through our affiliate links, the price you pay is identical to what you would pay navigating directly to the retailer. Commissions are paid by the retailer, not by you.
