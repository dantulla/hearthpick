Our story

We obsess so you don't have to.

Hearthpick started as a personal Pinterest board — a place to save the home finds that were actually worth buying, not just saving. It grew into something bigger when people started asking where we found things.

Every item on this site has been researched, vetted, and chosen because it genuinely makes a home better. No paid placements. No filler. No recommendations we wouldn't make to a close friend.

We earn a small commission when you shop through our links, at no extra cost to you. That's what keeps the lights on and the finds coming every week.

What we stand for

Our principles

✦

Honest Curation

We never feature something we wouldn't buy ourselves. Poor reviews, flimsy quality, or style over substance — it doesn't make the cut.

◎

Full Transparency

Every affiliate link is clearly disclosed. Commissions never influence our recommendations — we've turned down sponsored placements.

◈

Every Budget

Great homes aren't built on big budgets. We curate from $15 accent pieces to investment sofas — real life isn't one-size-fits-all.

❋

Weekly Freshness

Every Thursday we drop new finds across all categories, with a newsletter edit that goes to subscribers first.

◇

Real Research

We read reviews, check return rates, compare materials, and often order before recommending. We retire finds with poor long-term feedback.

○

Privacy First

We collect only what we need. We don't sell subscriber data. Ever. See our Privacy Policy.

Get in touch

Questions, collaborations, or just want to say hello? We read every email.
