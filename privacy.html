When you visit our site, we may automatically receive: browser type and version, pages visited and time spent, referring website, approximate geographic location (country/region only), and device type.

We use cookies for basic site functionality and analytics. We do not use advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may not function correctly.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share with: email service providers (contractually prohibited from using your data for their own purposes), analytics providers (aggregate, anonymised data only), and legal authorities (if required by law).

Our site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
